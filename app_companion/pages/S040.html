In this page you can add packingslips to the list by scanning or by keyboard. Each packingslip is validated by a webservice call.

Packingslips can be removed from the list by swiping a row to the left or right.

When you are done adding rows, you can press Ready to confirm the list to the backend.

You may press Reopen to add more packingslips to the list.
