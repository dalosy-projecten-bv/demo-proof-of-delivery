Main menu

Page id: S020

This is the main menu of the application.

The user can choose one of the following actions:

  • Loading
  • Unloading
  • Pick up

Loading

This function is used by the person in the warehouse who loads the truck.

Unloading

This function is used by the truck driver when delivering goods to the customer.

Pick up

This function is used by the person in the warehouse who is responsible for over the counter sales.
